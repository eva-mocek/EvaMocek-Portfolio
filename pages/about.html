Hello! I'm Eva
Fashion Designer and Front-End Dev from Toronto, ON

Fashion Design

My experience in the fashion industry ranges broadly, including everything from costume to bridalwear, knitwear and dresses to stage drapes and industrial equipment covers. My current project is my brand, Juniper & Eve, which I started in June 2021 to provide sustainable underwear options to people across the gender spectrum.

Artist

A maker at heart, I always have several personal projects in the works. In 2018, I created a gown covered in fabric waterlilies, and directed a creative photoshoot with the help of my good friend and photographer, Arkinography, and the lovely Diana as model. This will hopefully become the first in a series of natural-habitat-inspired costumes, to be made in future using only upcycled/repurposed fabrics. I'm also beginning to learn graphic design and am working on several smaller design projects, coming soon!

Environmental Education

Bachelor of Environmental Studies, Honours Co-op from the Univeristy of Waterloo. Trying to incorporate sustainability and ecological responsibility into lifestyle as well as artistic practices. Juniper & Eve uses sustainable material... Link to blog post about sustainability maybe?

Fashion Education

My sartorial education began when my mom taught me to sew as a child and I've been creating clothing, costumes and accessories ever since. I later decided I wanted to up my skills to a professional level, and in 2015, completed the Essential Skills for Fashion Certificate Program at George Brown College, while working at the Lower Ossington Theater and selling my costumes and corsetry on Etsy.
