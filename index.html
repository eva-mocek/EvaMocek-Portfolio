Artist, business-owner, outdoor enthusiast and front-end developer; dive in to Eva's world and explore her recent projects across many disciplines. Based in Ontario, Canada.

Business Owner

In June, 2021, Eva started a business called Juniper & Eve. Her brand creates sustainable underwear for people across the gender spectrum, and specializes in gender-affirming garments. Juniper & Eve was featured in an article for Harpers Bazaar Vietman, and was an official business partner of Pride Toronto in 2021.

Front-End Developer

In October 2022, Eva took an Intro to Coding course through SheCodes and fell in love with coding! She is currently working through the full range of SheCodes courses and is learning front-end web skills, such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, Responsive, Bootstrap and React. This portfolio is her final project for the SheCodes Responsive course, and you can see the source code on Eva's Github.

Fashion and Art

An artist-at-heart, Eva loves to create beautiful and useful things. Her sartorial practice began as a child when her mother taught her to sew. Afterwards, Eva went on to study at George Brown College, completing the Essential Skills for Fashion ConEd program in 2017. Aside from running her business, Eva also creates bridalwear, does embroidery and is working on a personal capsule wardrobe. Away from the sewing machine, she is exploring graphic design and hopes to take courses in both photography and animation in the future.

Environmental Studies

Eva completed her Bachelor of Environmental Studies at the Univeristy of Waterloo in 2013, graduating from the co-op program with honours. She continues to nurture her passion for the outdoors by attending lectures on sustainable fashion and business development, incorporating what she learns into her business and artistic practice. Eva is an avid hiker, canoeist and swimmer, and spends as much timeas possible out of doors.
